**AccessForge -- 1-click accessibility mod platform**

AccessForge makes it easy to find, install, and update screen reader mods for mainstream games. No manual file copying, no mod loader setup. Download the app, run it, pick a mod, click install. That's it.

It handles everything behind the scenes: finding your game, installing mod loaders, downloading dependencies, and keeping mods up to date. Works with NVDA on Windows 10 and 11.

Download: https://github.com/AccessForge/AccessForge/releases/latest

Windows might show a SmartScreen warning since the exe isn't code-signed yet. Click "More info", then "Run anyway".

**For mod developers:**

If you make accessibility mods and want to reach more players, publishing through AccessForge takes about 10 minutes. You keep your GitHub repo -- no transfers, no central registry, no gatekeeping. Just add a small manifest file to your repo, tag it, and your mod shows up in the app.

Getting started guide: https://github.com/AccessForge/AccessForge/blob/main/docs/getting-started.md

AccessForge is a community project -- not owned by any single person. Contributions welcome, whether that's building mods, reporting bugs, improving docs, or writing code.

GitHub: https://github.com/AccessForge
